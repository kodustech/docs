---
title: "Can I use my own LLM provider for code review"
description: "Bring your own API key and choose your preferred LLM provider for AI-powered code review with Kodus."
---

Yes. Kodus supports Bring Your Own Key (BYOK), letting you use your preferred LLM provider.

## How it works

1. Go to **Settings** → **BYOK** (Bring Your Own Key)
2. Enter your API key for your preferred provider
3. Kodus uses your key for all AI operations

## Why use your own provider

- **Data control** — requests go directly to your provider
- **Cost management** — use your existing billing relationship
- **Model choice** — pick the model that works best for your codebase
- **Compliance** — meet internal requirements for AI tool usage

## Self-hosted LLM providers

If you self-host Kodus, you can also use self-hosted LLM providers like:
- Novita
- Groq
- Together AI
- Fireworks AI

For setup guides, see the [Cookbook](/cookbook/en/novita).

For BYOK configuration, see [Bring Your Own Key](/how_to_use/en/byok).
